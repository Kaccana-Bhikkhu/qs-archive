About Ajahn Pasanno

Ajahn Pasanno, one of the founding abbots of Abhayagiri Monastery and now its Guiding Elder, excels at answering Dhamma questions.

Known for his clarity, wisdom, and compassionate approach, Ajahn Pasanno has been instrumental in bringing the Forest Tradition of Theravada Buddhism to the West.
