The New Ajahn Chah Biography
Ajahn Pasanno
Upāsikā Day, Apr. 21, 2018
Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery in Redwood Valley, California and online
3 sessions, 34 excerpts, 2:33:56 total duration
Show featured excerpts (4)

After playing a compilation of video footage of Ajahn Chah from the BBS documentaries, Ajahn Pasanno reads from Stillness Flowing by Ajahn Jayasaro, the newly published biography of Ajahn Chah. He focuses on the chapter “Out of Compassion,” which describes Ajahn Chah’s teachings to the lay community.
