Ajahn Pasanno
Retreat, Jan. 1, 2021 to Jan. 3, 2021

An online New Year’s retreat taught by Luang Por Pasanno from January 1 to January 3, 2021 (Malaysian time) organized by the Buddhadhamma Foundation and Bandar Utama Buddhist Society. 350 people in 19 countries participated.
