Ānāpānasati: Mindfulness of In-and-Out Breathing – Winter Retreat 2005, part 1
Ajahn Pasanno
Abhayagiri Winter Retreat, Jan. 4, 2005 to Jan. 22, 2005
Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery in Redwood Valley, California
19 sessions, 72 excerpts, 7:02:29 total duration
Show featured excerpts (14)

Ajahn Pasanno offered this series of Dhamma talks on the Ānāpānasati Sutta (MN 118) to the Abhayagiri community at the beginning of the 2005 Winter Retreat. These are the most detailed teachings on his favorite meditation technique.
